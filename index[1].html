About us

Connecting Australian buyers with experienced Asian manufacturing.

Harmony Timber Australia supports commercial buyers seeking dependable timber supply, custom processing and factory-direct manufacturing.

The Australian business provides local communication, quotations, order coordination and customer support. Production and export preparation are supported by established operations in China and Vietnam.

We work with builders, wholesalers, timber merchants, furniture manufacturers, joinery businesses and project buyers.
